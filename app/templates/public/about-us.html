OFFICE OF THE UNIVERSITY REGISTRAR

The Office of the University Registrar (OUR) is the repository of student records such as admission, enrolment, grades, and academic status. It is also responsible for the issuance of official documents such as transcript of records, diploma, and certifications. The OUR also provides services such as enrolment, graduation, and authentication of documents.
